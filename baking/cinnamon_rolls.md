# Cinnamon Rolls

## Yield

\~18 Rolls

## Ingredients

-   Rapid-rise yeast, 2 pkg
-   Neutral oil, \>1/2 cup
-   Egg, 1 count
-   Flour, 6-7 cups
-   Sugar, \~1 cup
-   Brown sugar, \~1/2 cup
-   Ground cinnamon, \~2-3 tbsp
-   Salt, 2 tsp
-   Butter, softened, 1/2 cup (1 stick)
-   Cream cheese frosting (see separate recipe)

## Preparation

-   In a bowl, activate yeast by combining with 1 tbsp sugar in 1/3 cups warm water.
-   In a large bowl, combine 1 2/3 cups warm water, 1/2 cup sugar, 2 tsp salt, 1/2 cup oil, the egg, and 2 cups flour. Beat well. Add yeast mixture. Gradually add 4-5 cups of flour. Knead.
-   Cover dough with oil, plastic wrap and a dishcloth. Set in a warm place to raise for 40 minutes. Punch down and let rest on counter for another 15 minutes.
-   Create a sugar mixture of \~1/2 cup sugar, \~1/2 cup brown sugar and \~2-3 tbsp cinnamon sugar. Set aside.
-   Roll dough out into a rectangle. Spread 1/2 cup butter across dough and cover with sugar mixture. Roll dough into a log and pinch the seams closed. Using a piece of string or dental floss, cut into 1.5in slices. Place the pieces in a greased pan.
-   Let slices rise to top of pan (20-30 min).

## Baking

-   Pre-heat oven to 325°F. Bake rolls for 15-20 min.
-   Increase temp to 350°F and bake for another 10-15min until golden brown.

## Serving

Spread cream cheese frosting over rolls as they come out of oven. Serve once cool enough to eat.

## Note(s)

-   Caramel roll variation (no frosting)
    -   In a pot, bring 1/4 lbs butter, 1/4 cup milk, 1/4 cup corn syrup to boil. Add 1 tbsp vanilla and remove from heat.
    -   Pour caramel into greased pan before placing cinnamon roll slices in pan. Bake rolls in caramel following the above instructions.
